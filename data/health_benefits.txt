Health Benefits Policy

The company provides comprehensive health insurance coverage.

Employees may add eligible dependents to the insurance plan.

Annual health checkups are covered by the company.

Mental wellness counseling services are available through approved providers.

Employees have access to telemedicine consultations.

Preventive healthcare programs are encouraged.

Medical reimbursement claims should be submitted within 30 days.

Health insurance cards are issued after onboarding.

Vaccination programs may be conducted periodically.

Additional wellness initiatives may be announced by HR.
