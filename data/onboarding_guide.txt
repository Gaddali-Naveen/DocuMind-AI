Employee Onboarding Guide

New employees must complete HR documentation on their first day.

Company laptops and access credentials will be provided during onboarding.

Employees must review all company policies before starting work.

Mandatory training programs must be completed within the first month.

Managers will conduct onboarding sessions with new hires.

Employees will receive access to internal communication platforms.

Introduction meetings with team members will be scheduled.

Employees should acknowledge receipt of company assets.

Questions regarding onboarding can be directed to HR.

Successful onboarding helps employees become productive quickly.
