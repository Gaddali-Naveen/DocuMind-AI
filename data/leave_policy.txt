Leave Policy

Employees are entitled to 18 annual leave days per calendar year.

Employees receive 12 sick leave days annually.

Unused annual leave may be carried forward up to 10 days into the next year.

Leave requests should be submitted at least 3 working days in advance.

Emergency leave requests may be approved by reporting managers.

Employees on probation are eligible for sick leave but not annual leave accrual.

Maternity leave is available according to company regulations.

Paternity leave is available for eligible employees.

Extended leave requests require HR approval.

Employees should update leave records through the HR portal.
