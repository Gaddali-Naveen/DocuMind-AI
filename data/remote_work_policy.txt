Remote Work Policy

Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week.

Managers may approve additional remote work days based on project requirements.

Employees must remain available during core working hours from 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM.

Company laptops must be used for all remote work activities.

Employees must connect through the corporate VPN when accessing internal systems.

Remote workers are responsible for maintaining a stable internet connection.

Video meetings should be attended with cameras enabled when required.

Sensitive company data must not be stored on personal devices.

Employees must report technical issues immediately.

Remote work privileges may be revoked for policy violations.
