Employee Work Policy

Employees are required to work 8 hours per day.

The standard working week consists of 5 working days from Monday to Friday.

Employees must mark attendance before 10:00 AM.

Overtime work must be approved by reporting managers.

Employees are expected to maintain professional conduct during working hours.

Company assets such as laptops and monitors must be used responsibly.

Employees should not share confidential company information with external parties.

Business casual dress code is applicable for office employees.

Work-from-home employees must maintain a productive work environment.

Repeated violation of company policies may result in disciplinary action.
