Travel Policy

Business travel requires prior approval from reporting managers.

Employees should use approved travel booking platforms.

Economy class travel is applicable for domestic travel.

Hotel expenses will be reimbursed according to company guidelines.

Travel expense reports must be submitted within 7 days of travel completion.

Receipts must be attached for reimbursement claims.

Personal expenses during business travel are not reimbursable.

International travel requires additional management approval.

Travel advances may be requested when necessary.

Employees must comply with local laws while traveling.
