Information Security Policy

Employees must use multi-factor authentication for company accounts.

Passwords must be changed every 90 days.

Sharing credentials is strictly prohibited.

Sensitive company data must not be stored on personal devices.

All security incidents must be reported within 24 hours.

Employees must lock their systems when away from their desks.

Downloading unauthorized software is not permitted.

USB devices should not be connected without approval.

Phishing emails should be reported immediately.

Violations of security policies may result in disciplinary action.
